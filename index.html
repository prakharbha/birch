The House

The Club at Birch House is not a hotel in the traditional sense. It is a small coastal residence with just six suites, designed to feel relaxed, elegant, and quietly personal.

The scale allows for a different kind of stay. Mornings unfold at your own pace. Evenings are unhurried.

The atmosphere is calm, with an emphasis on space, light, and comfort rather than formality.

For more than two decades, The Pillars Hotel was known for its understated luxury and personal service. Birch House continues that philosophy in a more private and residential form.

This is a place for those who prefer intimacy over scale, and discretion over display.

View the Suites

The Suites

Six suites. Each one designed with a sense of space that is increasingly rare.

The suites are intentionally generous in scale. Four junior suites offer over five hundred square feet of open, comfortable living, while two one-bedroom suites extend to approximately eleven hundred square feet, creating a true residential experience.

The proportions allow for a natural rhythm to the space. Areas to sit, to rest, and to unwind are distinct yet connected, with light and air moving easily throughout.

The bathrooms are equally considered — spacious, light-filled, and finished in clean white tones. Freestanding bathtubs and glass-enclosed showers create a sense of openness and comfort.

With only six suites, the experience remains calm, personal, and discrete.

The Experience

A slower way to begin the day

Mornings begin slowly, with light from the coast and the option to step out toward the beach just moments away. There is no schedule, only the rhythm you choose.

Afternoons move easily between the house and the nearby marina, where the energy of the water is present but never intrusive.

Evenings are calm and understated. The setting invites conversation, a glass of wine, or simply time to unwind in a space that feels private and removed.

A more intimate expression of the spirit that defined The Pillars.

The Location

The Location

Quietly positioned on Fort Lauderdale Beach

Birch House is set just steps from the beach, offering immediate access to the ocean while remaining tucked away from the busier areas nearby.

Located moments from the marina, the setting reflects the quiet movement of the water without ever feeling exposed or crowded. Restaurants, shops, and the energy of Fort Lauderdale are within easy reach, yet the house itself remains calm and discrete.

A setting that offers both connection and a sense of remove.
